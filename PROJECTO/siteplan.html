Final Project

Site Name

Proposed Site Name: RIDEME!

Reason for Name Selection: The name "RideMe" was chosen because it clearly represents the goal and focus of the website, which is to provide a seamless and competitive alternative to traditional car rental services. Unlike traditional car rentals, where customers must wait in long counter lines, RideMe simplifies the process by ensuring that the car is ready and waiting for you at a designated location. This convenience and efficiency make RideMe a great solution for travelers and commuters seeking a hassle-free experience. The website will highlight this unique approach and provide an easy platform for users to book their rides.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide a streamlined platform for users to easily book rental cars without the hassle of traditional car rental processes. RideMe aims to revolutionize the rental car experience by offering pre-arranged vehicles at convenient pickup locations, eliminating the need for waiting in counterlines. Additionally, the website will provide detailed information about available car models, pricing, and nearby locations, making it simple for users to find and reserve the perfect car for their needs. RideMe focuses on convenience, efficiency, and customer satisfaction.

Scenarios

  • How can I book a car quickly without waiting in line at a rental counter?
  • Where can I find the nearest pickup location for a RideMe vehicle?
  • What types of cars are available, and how do I compare their features and prices?
  • Is there a way to see my rental history or manage my bookings online?
